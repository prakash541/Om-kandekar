About Ahmedabad

Ahmedabad is the largest city in the state of Gujarat, India. It is known for its rich culture, history, and heritage. The city is located on the banks of the Sabarmati River and is home to the Sabarmati Ashram, which played a key role in India's independence movement.

Popular Attractions

  • Sabarmati Ashram
  • Kankaria Lake
  • Adalaj Stepwell
  • Sidi Saiyyed Mosque
  • Akshardham Temple
