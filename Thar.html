Mahindra Thar

The Mahindra Thar is a rugged off-road SUV designed for adventure and durability. It comes with a powerful engine, 4x4 drivetrain, and a bold design perfect for all terrains.

  • Engine: 2.2L mHawk Diesel / 2.0L Turbo Petrol
  • Transmission: Manual & Automatic
  • Drivetrain: 4x4
  • Seating: 4-seater
