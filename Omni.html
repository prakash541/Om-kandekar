Maruti Suzuki Omni

The Maruti Suzuki Omni is a microvan that was introduced in India in 1984. Known for its practicality and compact design, it became a popular choice for families and small businesses alike.

Specifications

  • Engine: 796cc, 3-cylinder
  • Power: 34 bhp
  • Fuel Type: Petrol
  • Transmission: 4-speed Manual
  • Seating Capacity: 5-8 persons
  • Mileage: Approx. 14-17 km/l
