About Ahilya Nagar

Ahilya Nagar, previously called Ahmednagar, is a historic district in the state of Maharashtra, India. It was recently renamed in honor of the great Maratha queen Ahilyabai Holkar, known for her leadership and dedication to public welfare.

Railway Station Information

The main railway station serving the district is Ahilya Nagar Railway Station with station code ANG. It connects the district to major cities and towns across India.
