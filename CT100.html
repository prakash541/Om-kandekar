Bajaj CT100

100 cc commuter motorcycle — simple, reliable, highly fuel-efficient

Overview

The Bajaj CT100 is a 100 cc, 4-stroke commuter bike produced by Bajaj Auto. Originally launched in 2004, it was relaunched in 2015 and updated to meet BS6 norms in 2020. Known for its sturdy build and outstanding fuel economy, this motorcycle achieves up to ~89–90 km/L 1.

Specifications

Engine Type Single-cylinder, 4‑stroke, air‑cooled (≈99.3 cc)
Power ≈8.1–8.2 PS @ 7 500 rpm
Torque ≈8.05 Nm @ 4 500 rpm
Transmission 4‑speed manual
Top Speed ≈90 km/h
Fuel Economy ≈89–90 km/L
Fuel Tank ≈10.5 L
Curb Weight ≈108–115 kg
Front Suspension Hydraulic telescopic fork
Rear Suspension Trailing-arm with SNS (spring-in-spring)
Brakes 110 mm drum (front & rear)
Wheels/Tires 2.75×17″ front, 3.00×17″ rear (spoke or alloy)
