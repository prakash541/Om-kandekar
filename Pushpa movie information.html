Pushpa: The Rise (2021)

Director: Sukumar

Starring:

  • Allu Arjun as Pushpa Raj
  • Rashmika Mandanna as Srivalli
  • Fahadh Faasil as SP Bhanwar Singh Shekhawat

Language: Telugu

Release Date: December 17, 2021

Genre: Action, Drama

Synopsis

Pushpa: The Rise follows the story of Pushpa Raj, a laborer who rises in the world of red sandalwood smuggling in the Seshachalam forests of South India. Battling ruthless enemies and internal politics, Pushpa fights for his identity and place in the world.
